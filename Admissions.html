Admissions

Parijnan Vidyalay follows the Syllabus set by the Karnataka State Board.

We prescribe text books published by the NCERT.

PRE-NURSERY

Dates of interactive sessions of short listed candidates will be informed by SMS and e-mail only. Due to the limited number of seats available, all applicants may not be accommodated. Hence only short listed candidates will be informed along with further detailed instructions by SMS and e-mail only.

NOTE: Residence should be within 3 km radius.This school is for NOIDA residents only. No form will be issued or received after the due date.

[LINK: Download Admission Forms, Documents required list]

NURSERY-KG

Parents to apply for admission to Classes KG to V in the month of February-March. This is against any vacancy in the said class. If the child is short listed, he/she will be called for an admission test in the month of March/April.

[LINK: Download Admission Forms, Documents required list]

CLASS I TO IV 

Application forms are available only if there is a vacancy. Parents to apply for admission to Classes VI to IX in the month of February-March .If the child is short listed, he/she will be called for an admission test in English, Mathematics, Hindi and Science in the month of March/April.

[LINK: Download Admission Forms, Documents required list]

CLASS XI and XII

    SCIENCE STREAM

            Compulsory Subjects: English (Core), Chemistry, Physics, Mathematics.

            Optional Subjects: Biology, Computer Science, Economics, Psychology, Commercial             Art,     Physical Education. 

    COMMERCE STREAM

            Compulsory Subjects:  English (Core), Business Studies, Economics, Accountancy.  

            Optional Subjects:  Mathematics /Psychology /Commercial Art/Physical Education. 

    HUMANITIES

             Compulsory Subjects: English (Core), Political Science, History.              Economics/Geography/ Mathematics/  

            Optional Subjects: Psychology/Physical Education/ Commercial Art/ Computer             Science.
