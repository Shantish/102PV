Fulfilling the local needs for a high quality, value-based, English-medium High School, the Sāraswat Education Society initiated the Parijnan Vidyalay Pre-primary School in 2017. The expansion of Parijnan Vidyalay to a full-fledged English-medium High School conforming to CBSC curriculum is now being executed in a phased manner.    

In 2018, the pre-primary building was renovated and one section each of Std I – IV classes added. A sharp rise in registrations for admission to Lower Kindergarten – Std IV standard was noticed within the first 2 years itself. Noting this encouraging trend, the Sāraswat Education Society made plans to expand its infrastructure up to X standard in two phases. Rooms for Music, Dance, Arts and Sports along with a Counselling/Medical room are planned. On completion of the expansion, there will be 2 sections each for classes from Nursery to X Standard with a cumulative capacity of 900 students every year.

Once ready, this full-fledged, modern, well-equipped, English-medium Secondary School, is expected to benefit the low to medium income households in and around Koṭekar. The school will lay emphasis on arts, culture and heritage apart from science, commerce and digital literacy. Local community sensitivities will be taken care of through frequent interactions for providing quality, value-based education.
